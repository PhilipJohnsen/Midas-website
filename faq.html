    Frequently asked questions

    How do I return the jewelery

    When you receive your package there is a piece of paper inside. When you want to return your package you simply stick the piece of paper on the outside of the box you received your jewelery in. And then just send your jewelery back in the same box it came in so we are assured our product doesn't get damaged

    What happends if the jewelery arrives in damaged condition?

    If the jewelery arrives at your home in damaged condition we will pick it up from your home to assure that it doesn't get damaged further and we will give you a 30% discount on your next rental from midas jewelery

    What happends if I break the jewelery while wearing it?

    This deppends on whether you bought insurence or not. If you purchased insurence with your Midas rental then nothing will happend. we will pick up the broken piece of jewelery. If you haven't purchased the insurence you will be forced to pay the full price of the product so we can replace it.

    Do i recieve some information about the product, so i can answer if asked about the product?

    In every box there is a piece of paper with some knowledge about your rental. You will be able to know: How old it is, how much it costs, the brand and so on
